About the author

Lizzy Ellbrück (b. 1990, Ulm, DE) lives and works in Berlin. She studied at TU Berlin, UdK Berlin, and HfG Karlsruhe. In 2023, she received a scholarship from the State Graduate Funding Program, and in 2024/25 she was a resident at the Cité internationale des arts in Paris. She also participated in the Kunstbüro mentoring program in tandem with Sofia Hultén. Her works have been shown in solo exhibitions at venues including the Badischer Kunstverein Karlsruhe, the ZKM Pavilion, and the Kunstverein Bruchsal (with Johanna Schäfer), as well as in international group exhibitions.

Props Promising Prompts
Lizzy Ellbrück & Louise Raspé

Props Promising Prompts is a piece built in fragments: obstacles, niche wanderers, props, fillers, flourishes, and wandering eyes. Figures appear as embedded characters in a spatial narrative, thinking in ciphers, speaking in curlicues, writing in parentheses. They are not theatrical in that sense, yet they emerge at the edge of a drama — within footnotes, in the traces of scenes and marginalia.

About the authors

Lizzy Ellbrück and Louisa Raspé are artists, authors, designers, dramaturges, scenographers, accomplices and friends. They work artistically and speculatively, preferably in dialogue. Their studies and shared interests have fostered a rich and evolving complicity — an intensity they seek to deepen in Conceptual Conversations, meandering through new stories that unfold in several directions at once.
